REMARKS

This compilation covers year 2014.
This compilation covers the countries, which were selected in the IFA & IPNI report.
This compilation divides crops into 14 groups corresponding to the groups in the IFA & IPNI report. 

Metric tonnes of P2O5 per annum are used for volumes throughout this compilation.
Original data values are recorded in thousand metric tonnes of P2O5 in the IFA & IPNI assessment of fertilizer use.
