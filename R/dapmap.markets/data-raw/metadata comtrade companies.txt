REMARKS

For the purpose of this document, the following definitions apply:
DAP - diammonium phosphate,
MAP - monoammonium phosphate.

This compilation covers the period from 2013 to 2017.
Countries are reported by ISO 3166 country code.

This compilation covers the following companies and groups of companies:
China (export)		Chinese exporters (comtrade entries for China)
Maaden			Maaden (comtrade entries for Saudi Arabia)
Mosaic			Mosaic (comtrade entries for the United States)
PhosAgro/EuroChem	PhosAgro, EuroChem (comtrade entries for Russia, Lithunia)
OCP			OCP (comtrade entries for Morocco)

Metric tonnes of P2O5 per annum are used for trade volumes throughout this compilation.
Original data values are recorded in kilograms.
The following conversion factors of product to P2O5 tonnes were applied:
DAP		0.46
MAP		0.52
MAP (China)	0.44
